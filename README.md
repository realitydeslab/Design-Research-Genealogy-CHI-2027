# Design Research Genealogy: Methodologies and Works

**Author:** Botao Amber Hu  
**Venue:** CHI 2027 Papers  
**Deadline:** 2026-09-12  
**Method:** PRISMA Systematic Scoping Review  

## Abstract

A systematic scoping review to construct a Design Research Genealogy — mapping all design research methods and the research works that employ them. We draw a genealogy of lineages from Research through Design (RtD) to more-than-human design, speculative design, and futuring methods.

## Approach

PRISMA-based systematic scoping review of design research methodologies in HCI, design studies, and related fields. The genealogy traces methodological lineages, influences, and evolution across:

- Research through Design (RtD)
- More-than-human design
- Speculative design
- Critical design
- Design fiction
- Participatory design
- Co-design
- Futuring methods
- Adversarial design
- Research through Art
